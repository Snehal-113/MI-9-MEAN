The most interesting fact about the Peacock is the colorful features of this pheasant family. They can include colors of black, green, blue, gray, red, and orange. They can be displayed in a train that travels behind the body of the bird. The markings can be very different for each Peacock too. There are three species of this bird. They are among the easiest species of birds to raise so they are often kept as pets. INTERFACE.
